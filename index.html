James Milton is a designer, developer and creator from the United Kingdom, currently residing in Salzburg, Austria.

He is currently working as a Digital Product Specialist at Red Bull Media House, after recently completing his scholarship funded Masters in Digital and Interactive Design at the University of Leeds Design School. Previous to that, he graduated from the University of Sheffield with a First-class honours degree in Computer Science, fulfilled a yearlong Software Engineer internship with Rolls-Royce and formed a working partnership with Arup Design.

In addition to exploring design and code, James is a keen videoographer and self-taught guitarist. More of his work can be found on his portfolio.

Take a look at his résumé. If you like what you see, send over an email, or find him on instagram, youtube or linkedin.
