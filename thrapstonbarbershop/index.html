About Us

Opened in August 2017 by founders Jonny and Ryan. They built a talented team renowned for providing the best styles in mens grooming. Thrapston Barbershop welcomes gents of all ages to experience our upbeat chilled atmosphere in the ideal man cave. Offering music, complimentary drinks and TV with a gaming console for all clients to enjoy the ultimate haircut. At the heart of the shop is a passion and the theme for motorcycles. Besides providing the optimum traditional barbering experience and following on from the motorcycle theme; RJB also specialise in buying and selling second hand motorcycles, parts as well as clothing. The RJB collection ranges from classic Japanese Honda’s to ex-military enduro bikes that gives the shop real character and its distinct unique vibe for all guys to enjoy and hang out.

If you would like to come to our shop please feel free to walk in. We also offer appointments once a week on Thursdays.
