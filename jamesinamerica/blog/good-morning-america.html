Good Morning America

11 o'clock (New York time), 4 o'clock (England time), was my first sleep of the trip. I've just got up now to go for run around Central Park, which is only 4 blocks away.

Last night we landed at JFK and got a yellow taxi to our hotel. Seeing as there is 4 of us, and there are only 3 seats in the back of the taxi, I sat in the front!

My family were quite Jet-lagged last night, but my dad and I decided to go for a walk to Times Square, which was only 5 blocks away. Unfortunately I only had my iPhone camera, so the shots I took weren't that great, but I'll get some better ones tonight.

In my last post, I said that I'll be doing a blog post on the plane. Turns out you had to pay for it, screw that...
